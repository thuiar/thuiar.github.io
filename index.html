Intent Recognition for Human-Machine Interactions

The natural interaction ability between human and machine mainly involves humanmachine dialogue ability, multi-modal sentiment analysis ability, human-machine cooperation ability, and so on. In order to realize the efficient dialogue ability of intelligent computer, it is necessary to make the computer own strong user intention understanding ability in the process of human-computer interaction. This is one of the key technologies to realize efficient and intelligent human-computer dialogue. Currently, the understanding of the objects to be analyzed requires different levels of ability such as recognition, cognition, and reasoning. The current research on human-computer interaction intention understanding is still focused on the level of recognition. The research and application of human-computer natural interaction intention recognition mainly includes the following levels: intention classification, unknown intention detection, and open intention discovery. Intention understanding for natural interaction is a comprehensive research field involving the integration of natural language processing, machine learning, algorithms, human-computer interaction, and other aspects. In recent years, our research team from State Key Laboratory of Intelligent Technology and Systems, Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University has conducted a lot of pioneering research and applied work, which have been carried out in the field of intention understanding for natural interaction, especially in the field of intention classification, unknown intention detection and open intention discovery based on text information of human-machine dialogue based on deep learning models. Related achievements have also been published in the top academic international conferences in the field of artificial intelligence in recent years, such as ACL, AAAI, ACM MM, and well-known international journals, such as Pattern Recognition and Knowledge-based Systems. In order to systematically present the latest achievements in intention classification, unknown intention detection, and open intention discovery in academia in recent years, the relevant work achievements are systematically sorted out and presented to readers in the form of a complete systematic discussion. Currently, the research on intention understanding in natural interaction develops quickly. The author’s research team will timely sort out and summarize the latest achievements and share them with readers in the form of a series of books in the future. Natural Interaction for Tri-Co Robots (3) Named Entity Recognition and Relationship Extraction

Cognitive robots are robots capable of natural interaction with their operating environment, humans, and other robots, adapting autonomously to complex dynamic environments and collaborating in tasks. 'Sensitive and considerate' natural interaction is one of the key research topics in the field of cognitive service robots. There is currently an urgent need for cognitive robots to possess the ability to understand complex semantic information. This book is rooted in the field of information and knowledge extraction based on deep learning methods, starting from text representation learning. It systematically introduces methods for extracting named entities and entity relationships from knowledge information in the real world and further explores how to achieve robust entity relationship analysis in open domains. This book is the first domestic systematic introduction to deep learning-based named entity recognition and relationship extraction in the field of natural interaction for cognitive robots. It provides readers with key technologies and foundational knowledge in the field of information and knowledge extraction in the cognitive robotics research domain, offering important learning and research references to keep up with the cutting edge of this field. Multi objective Bayesian Optimization

It is the first academic monograph in China to systematically elaborate the solutions of expensive multi-objective Bayesian optimization problems. The Gauss process proxy model and acquisition function strategy are used to solve the problems of low dimensional parallel evaluation and high dimensional disaster, and improve the optimization efficiency. Features of this book: In depth multi-level research: comprehensively explore the practical application of various representative multi-objective intelligent optimization methods in various fields, provide practical guidance and solutions for professionals, and help with problem solving and intelligent decision-making. Algorithms inspired by nature: learn from the wisdom of biological systems, adopt bionic intelligent optimization algorithms, break through traditional mathematical analysis methods, solve expensive multi-objective optimization problems, and provide unique optimization solutions. Focus on high-dimensional multi-objective Bayesian Optimization: focus on the challenges in high-dimensional target space, introduce advanced algorithms and technologies, including the auxiliary optimization of target dimensionality reduction agent model, and provide key methods and tools for dealing with complex intelligent decision-making problems in the real world.
